🔍 HOW IT WORKS
───────────────
1. Find anchor's spine node (or climb from leaf to junction)
2. BFS from spine → find farthest leaf A
3. BFS again (avoid A's direction) → find farthest leaf B
4. Concatenate: [...pathA.reverse(), spine, ...pathB]
5. Trim to 900px window centered on anchor
6. Resample with 64-point minimum guarantee
7. Lock route (never change at runtime)
